Pairs League 2020

Why it's happening

With the cancellation of competitive bowls this year, as a club we decided to create a fun, internal pairs competition, to get people back on the green.

How it works

37 players registered an interest in playing. We drew 11 squads of 3 players and 1 squad of 4 players. The teams were divided into 2 leagues, playing their opponents twice.

Who will be champion

The two league winners will play a final to be crowned Wroughton BC Covid Pairs Champions.
