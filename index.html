What competitions are we running this year and why?

Singles competitions

The club have arranged three singles competitions to be played this year, men's singles, ladies singles and an open single's. The men's and ladies champions are eligable for the National Champion of Champions 2021.

Pairs league

Two leagues of 6 teams will compete to be crowned champions. After playing their opponents twice each, the two league winners will face in a battle to be crowned the pairs league champions.

Why are we doing this?

Due to the leagues being cancelled this season, as soon at restrictions were lifted, as a club we wanted to give those that were interested the chance to bowl. After a few weeks of roll ups only, it was decided to run a few a competitions to allow some competitive bowls to take place.

About

WROUGHTON BOWLS CLUB is a friendly village club in the heart of Wiltshire.
